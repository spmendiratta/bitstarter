Admittedly, this is a very simple idea. It clearly falls in the category of small business, not a startup. It is hard to envision this business growing fast (key attribute of startups). Another attribute of successful startups, world domination, is not even remotely our intent. We see this business growing, at best, at a glacial pace.

Motivation:
More than viability of the project, the objective is to understand the key technologies involved in deployment of a modern ecommerce website. In addition, it will be interesting to see how this website can be promoted on the internet using Google or Facebook market analytics tools.

Differentiators:
 _ Bedrock commitment to fair trade
_ Promotion of indigenous crafts
_ Evolution as a market place for ethnic hats

FAQ

What is a 'topi' ?
'Topi' in India and many South Asian countries means a hat in all its wonderful variations. For sake of completeness, topi also means a sub-Saharan antelope - that is purely coincidental, not our focus at all.
What does this business aspire to be ?
A portal for all topi aficionados.
